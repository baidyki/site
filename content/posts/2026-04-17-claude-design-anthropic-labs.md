Anthropic [launched Claude Design](https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-design-anthropic-labs), a tool for creating visual work — designs, prototypes, slides, and marketing materials — through conversation with Claude. Available at [claude.ai/design](https://claude.ai/design), it is powered by Claude Opus 4.7 and available as a research preview for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise subscribers. The tool ingests codebases and design files to build brand-consistent design systems automatically, applying existing colors, typography, and components. Input can come from text prompts, uploaded DOCX/PPTX/XLSX files, or captured website elements; output can be exported to Canva, PDF, PPTX, or HTML, or handed off directly to Claude Code for development.

The pitch is time compression for design-adjacent work: Datadog reports that work previously taking a week now happens in a single conversation. Canva is integrating directly so assets created in Claude Design can flow into Canva without friction. The product sits between quick AI generation and full design tools — targeted at teams that need realistic prototypes and pitch decks fast, not at designers who need precise control.

Key points:

- **Powered by Opus 4.7** — research preview, currently limited to paid Claude tiers.
- **Brand-aware by default** — reads codebases and design files to apply existing visual systems.
- **Full export chain** — Canva, PDF, PPTX, HTML, and direct Claude Code handoff.
- **Inline refinement** — comments, direct text edits, and spacing/color/layout controls without regenerating from scratch.
